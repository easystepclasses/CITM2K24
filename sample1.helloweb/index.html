Inter College Competition CITM-2K24: Youth Fest

Compucom Institute of Technology and Management is thrilled to host CITM-2K24: Youth Fest, an exhilarating inter-college competition celebrating talent, creativity, and innovation on 6th and 7th December 2024.

Event Highlights

  • Sports Events: Showcase your skills in Volleyball, Table Tennis, Carrom, and Chess.
  • Cultural Events: Unleash your creativity with Mehendi Design, Rangoli, and Poster Design.
  • Technical Events: Ignite your intellect in the Quiz, present innovations in the Project Display, or share knowledge through Paper Presentations.

Join us for two days of spirited competition and camaraderie. Let the best talents shine!
